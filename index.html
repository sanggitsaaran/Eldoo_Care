About Us

Who We Are & Why We Do This

Vision

To create a healthy world where every parent ages with dignity — supported by a lifelong promise of seamless, and compassionate care. Our north star is simple: a world where no one is left to age alone. Where children can be present — even from a distance — and where every older adult can live with the safety, comfort, and respect they deserve.

Mission

We bridge the gap between home and hospital so you are never alone. Whether it is clinical care delivered in your living room or a safe, dignified escort to the specialist — Eldoo Care is your constant companion. We are the steadfast presence that turns worry into certainty, for both the parent and the child.

Brand Promise

Safety for them. Certainty for you. We make your home a safe place to heal and our vehicles a safe way to travel. With smart technology and a genuinely human touch, we stay by your side — from your home to the hospital and back.

Our Story

Founded With Purpose & Passion

Omprakash — Co-founder, Eldoo Care

Eldoo Care was founded by people who have lived the helplessness of watching people need care from a distance — and who refused to accept that nothing better existed.

Omprakash graduated from the London School of Economics (LSE), with a decade of leadership across the civil service, multilateral organizations, and the corporate sector, he has built the infrastructure that makes societies more inclusive. From the Indian Space Research Organisation to the United Nations, his career is defined by one discipline: transforming the impossible-to-execute into the impossible-to-ignore.

The helplessness of managing his own parents’ care from a distance exposed a systemic failure in eldercare. Refusing to accept ‘caregiver guilt,’ Omprakash co-founded Eldoo Care to engineer a future of institutional trust. By professionalizing the 'First and Last Miles' of the care journey, he ensures families stay connected, regardless of the distance.
